The Fact of Eating Mud Crab (Ketam Nipah)

According to Bahagian Dakwah (Jabatan Kemajuan Islam Malaysia), they have discussed and viewed that Muslims can eat mud crab as it is considered as halal. To their points of view, mud crab is categorised with the same group of prawn and clam. Mud crab mostly spends its life in the water and it breathes with gills but not lungs in the water.
